Exam advice

  1. Always bring a Highlighter, it's imporant to Highlight the important information. This rule is to make sure you read it properly, you can always underline instead. You may think of course I don't need to, you do, you may only lose a few marks but a few marks here and there make a grade, the amount of times ive misread the question and seen an L turn into a 1, a 69 to 96 and 5mA to 5A is unbelivable! All solved with the simple highlighter.But don't do this clearly this is useless!
  2. Never leave a question blank, you can always scrape a few marks by just trying, however if you're stuck and don't know leave it, and return to it later. This feeds into 4.
  3. Read the entire paper, you're sometimes given hints on other questions that can help you answer them(if you don't know already).
